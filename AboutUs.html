About Us

WHAT WE DO?


Our mission is to elevate childhood. We are growing a technology-enabled network of leaders that bring families together in a natural place in their community for classes where kids learn through play. Each class becomes a tight-knit group of children and adults who learn together through well-designed, outdoor play-based activities.

We help kids develop a host of important capabilities, including empathy, collaboration, creativity, persistence and problem solving. Our expert-designed classes and activities help kids ages 18 months to 8 years develop core life skills while enjoying healthy, fun, engaging experiences in the physical freedom of local green spaces.
